INGREDIENTS

  • non-stick cooking spray
  • 1(10.5oz) can condensed cream of chicken soup
  • 2 cups greek yogurt
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon black pepper
  • 2 cups shredded cheese
  • 1(30oz)package hashbrowns
  • 1/4 cup butter, melted

STEPS

  1. Preheat oven to 350F
  2. Whisk together sour cream, condensed soup, salt, and pepper in a large bowl. Stir in cheese and hash browns, spoon evenly into prepared baking dish.
  3. Pour butter over the casserole.
  4. Bake uncovered until hot and bubbly, 45 to 50 minutes. Remove from the oven and let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  5. And that's it. Enjoy!
